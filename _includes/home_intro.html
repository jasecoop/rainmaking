Startupbootcamp started in 2010 and now operates globally with 13 industry focused startup accelerators. We help startups scale globally by giving them direct access to an international network of the most relevant partners, investors and mentors in their sector.

Over the last ten years, we’ve created a global community of startups, bringing entrepreneurs, investors and corporate partners together. Our coworking spaces in Berlin, Copenhagen and London are now home to over 1,000 people – and there’s always room for new arrivals.

Expect hackathons, lively debates, new ideas and networking events with industry and government figures. In our co-working spaces around the world, we run a regular events on a variety of subjects, from fintech to cyber security, startup to corporate.
